About

At Ayinmiro's Construction Services we provide a range of building consulting services for commercial, residential, & industrial projects.  Our 40 years of experience and understanding of these projects from conception through completion creates project efficiencies and foresight not found at other firms.  We have a proven track record and over ten years of experience.We offer multiple services designed to fit your needs.  Our approach is to work closely with a number of experienced professionals to provide comprehensive project planning, execution, and construction solutions for any project.   We pride ourselves on being honest and accountable to our clients while guiding them through the construction process.  Our mission, utilizing this approach, is to bring your projects in on time and budget.  We believe our approach provides our clients with the best possible outcomes.
